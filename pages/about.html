About Me

Hello! I’m Noelle Bhaduri, the heart and hands behind No Bhad Dogs. I’ve been working professionally in pet care since 2015, but my journey with dogs began long before that.

With over 30 years of personal experience caring for dogs of all breeds, sizes, and temperaments, I bring a deep understanding of canine behavior, needs, and personalities. Whether it’s a high-energy pup or a senior dog needing special attention, I tailor my care to each dog’s unique needs.

My services are grounded in compassion, reliability, and trust. I’ve built lasting relationships with both pets and their people, and I take pride in being someone you can count on — whether for a daily walk, an overnight stay, or administering medications with care and confidence.

Thank you for considering me to care for your furry family members. I look forward to meeting you and your dog!
